Image Mask Creator

Generate button is at the button of the page. If quantity is more than 10, it could take a few seconds to generate.
The Position Distribution Characterstics will take some playing around with (notes below).

  • min and max: percentage (0 to 1) of screen to place image
  • mean: VERY IMPORTANT - the average percentage of the screen where most images will be placed (0.5 is the center)
  • standard deviation: VERY IMPORTANT - basically how spread out generation is from mean.
    ====> 0.1 is towards center
    ====> 1 is basically completely random
  • skew: not as important, but makes generation skew to a direction
